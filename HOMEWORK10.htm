The RB26DETT is a legendary inline-six engine produced by Nissan, famed for its use in the Nissan Skyline GT-R (R32, R33, and R34 models). This 2.6-liter twin-turbocharged powerhouse was built with performance and reliability in mind, featuring a cast-iron block, aluminum cylinder head, and dual overhead cams. It became a symbol of Japanese automotive engineering, capable of producing impressive horsepower even in stock form, while also offering immense tuning potential for enthusiasts. The RB26DETT remains an icon among car enthusiasts and a cornerstone of JDM car culture.
